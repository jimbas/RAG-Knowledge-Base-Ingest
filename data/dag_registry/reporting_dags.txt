## DAG: report_daily_revenue

Purpose: Aggregates daily revenue from BigQuery, generates PDF report, emails to finance
Schedule: 0 7 * * * (daily at 07:00 UTC)
Owner: analytics-team
Criticality: High — finance team reviews before 09:00 UTC daily
Tags: reporting, bigquery, email

Tasks:
- query_revenue_bq: BigQueryOperator pulls revenue aggregates for previous day
- build_report: PythonOperator uses matplotlib/reportlab to generate PDF
- email_report: EmailOperator sends PDF to finance distribution list

Common Failures:
- query_revenue_bq: fails if etl_customer_orders did not complete by 07:00 UTC
- build_report: OOMKilled if previous day had unusually high transaction volume
- email_report: SMTP authentication failure if email credentials rotated

Dependencies: requires etl_customer_orders to succeed first
On Failure: notify #analytics-alerts, directly email analytics-lead@company.com

## DAG: report_weekly_inventory

Purpose: Generates weekly inventory health report for supply chain review
Schedule: 0 8 * * 1 (every Monday at 08:00 UTC)
Owner: supply-chain-team
Criticality: Low
Tags: reporting, postgres, email

Tasks:
- query_inventory: PostgresOperator aggregates inventory.current_levels
- build_excel_report: PythonOperator generates Excel file using openpyxl
- upload_to_s3: PythonOperator uploads report to S3 reports bucket
- notify_team: SlackOperator posts report link to #supply-chain channel

Common Failures:
- upload_to_s3: AWS credentials expired or S3 bucket policy changed
- notify_team: Slack token revoked

On Failure: notify #supply-chain-alerts
